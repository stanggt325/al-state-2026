Welcome to Alabama

Major match energy. Shooter-built experience.

The 2026 USPSA Alabama State Championship brings competitors together at the CMP Talladega Marksmanship Park for a fast, challenging, and professionally run match built around safety, flow, and fun.

Hosted by shooters, for shooters.

On behalf of the Alabama USPSA Section, Talladega Practical Shooters, and CMP Talladega, thank you for joining us. A great deal of time and effort has gone into the preparation, setup, and organization of this match. Please be safe, help the staff keep the match moving, and most of all — have fun.

Alabama in June is not subtle.

Expect sun, heat, humidity, and limited shade. Hydrate before, during, and after the match. Complimentary bottled water will be available in each bay.
